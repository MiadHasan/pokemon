1. Pikachu: Ash's first and most loyal Pokémon, Pikachu is an Electric-type Pokémon and Ash's main companion throughout his journey. Pikachu is known for its strong bond with Ash and its powerful Electric-type moves like Thunderbolt and Electro Ball.

2. Charizard: Originally a Charmander, this Fire/Flying-type Pokémon evolved into Charizard, becoming one of Ash's most powerful and iconic Pokémon. Charizard is known for its fiery temperament and immense battling skills.

3. Bulbasaur: Ash caught Bulbasaur in the Kanto region. It is a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon and is known for its strong sense of leadership and its caring nature towards other Pokémon.

4. Squirtle: Another one of Ash's original Pokémon captured in the Kanto region, Squirtle is a Water-type Pokémon known for its mischievous personality and strong sense of justice.

5. Pidgeot: Originally a Pidgeotto, this Flying-type Pokémon evolved under Ash's care. Pidgeot is known for its speed and aerial agility, often serving as Ash's mode of transportation in the early stages of his journey.

6. Snorlax: Ash captured Snorlax in the Orange Islands. This Normal-type Pokémon is known for its immense size and appetite, as well as its formidable strength in battle.

7. Heracross: Ash caught Heracross in the Johto region. This Bug/Fighting-type Pokémon is known for its determination and powerful fighting moves.

8. Sceptile: Originally a Treecko, this Grass-type Pokémon evolved into Grovyle and then into Sceptile during Ash's journey in the Hoenn region. Sceptile is known for its speed, agility, and powerful Grass-type moves.

9. Infernape: Ash's Chimchar evolved into Monferno and then into Infernape during his journey in the Sinnoh region. Infernape is a Fire/Fighting-type Pokémon known for its fiery spirit and powerful fighting moves.

10. Greninja: Ash's Froakie evolved into Frogadier and then into Greninja during his journey in the Kalos region. Greninja is a Water/Dark-type Pokémon known for its speed, agility, and unique ability, Battle Bond.
